 Climate Trend Analyzer

 Project Overview

The Climate Trend Analyzer is a data science project designed to analyze historical climate data, identify long-term trends, detect anomalies, and forecast future climate conditions.



 Problem Statement

Climate data is complex and difficult to interpret manually. This project provides a structured approach to uncover patterns, seasonal variations, and anomalies in climate data.



 Industry Relevance
Used in:

* Climate research organizations
* Government policy planning
* Environmental monitoring
* Smart city development

 Business / Research Value

* Predict extreme weather events
